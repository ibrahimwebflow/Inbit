How Inbit Works

Our simple 3-step process ensures quality work and secure transactions for both clients and freelancers

1. Post & Match

Clients post jobs with required skills. Our system automatically matches with qualified freelancers.

2. Hire & Collaborate

Review matches, hire the best fit, and collaborate through our secure chat and milestone system.

3. Deliver & Release

Freelancer submits work, admin quality checks, then client releases payment upon satisfaction.
